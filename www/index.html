My Life in Slides

How I Became Myself
By: Nicholas Holes

Math

I have always been very interested in mathematics growing up. This was a result of it being one of the first things in life I realized I was exceptionally good at.

Snowboarding

I have been lucky enough that my parents moved right next to a small ski hill called Chicopee. I have been snowboarding for over 10 years now and look forward to Winter every year just to snowboard. This is a video of my favourite snowboarding series.

Video Games

Gaming has become one of my favourite activities because I can connect with all my friends and be immsersed in fantasy worlds. My favourite game is League of Legends because of how competitive and fun it is.

Streetwear

I fell in love with expressing myself by my choice of clothing since it is the best way to differentiate yourself. My favourite brand is Palace, a skateboarding brand from the UK that has gone mainstream.

Conclusion

My love for math has helped me develop my problem solving ability, my passion for snowboarding has taught me to get back up after I fall, my countless hours spent playing games has taught me to work hard and play hard, and finally, my interest in fashion has taught me to not be afraid to express myself.
